Archimede6.com
A brand new portfolio for a comunication fashion agency in Milan.

For my Master thesis research, I investigated how the human being has systematically been re-designing itself through the act of design. I built an interactive device which simulates how the human body would evolve to adapt to certain environmental changes set up by the users. The machine produces a generative output in the form of a small A4 size poster which contains the different parts of the adapted human body. The project took part in the Speculative Design exhibition ‚ÄúWhat Makes Humans Human?‚Äù held at BASE Milano in March 2019.
