Archimede6
A brand new portfolio for a communication fashion agency in Milan.

Visit website üîó

Archimede6 is a fashion communication agency based in Milan that deals with art direction, production, shooting, digital strategy, visual and web design. In 2020 they launched their new website and I took care of its web development while working closely with designers. The challenge was to present all the services that the studio offers in a clear way while communicating effectively its identity. With this in mind, the website has been developed to optimise user navigation through the use of multiple interaction effects that enhance the visitor experience.
